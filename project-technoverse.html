Project Overview

TechnoVerse is a dedicated website built to enhance the experience of a college technical fest by serving as a central digital hub for interactive games, challenges, and technology-driven activities. The platform encourages students to learn, compete, and have fun simultaneously—making the event more engaging and memorable.

Live Demo (Event Only) GitHub Repo (Private)

Problem & Solution

The Problem

Traditional college tech events often rely on offline coordination and scattered information sources, making it difficult for participants to discover activities, track challenges, or stay engaged throughout the event.

The Solution

TechnoVerse consolidates all event-related activities into a single interactive platform. Participants can explore technology-themed games, coding challenges, quizzes, and fun activities through an intuitive interface—boosting engagement and participation.
